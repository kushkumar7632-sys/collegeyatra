Government Engineering College, Nawada
About College

Government Engineering College (GEC), Nawada is a government technical institution located in Nawada. It was established in 2019 by the Department of Science and Technology Bihar to expand engineering education across Bihar.
The college is affiliated with Bihar Engineering University, Patna and is approved by AICTE.

Available Branches
  • Computer Science Engineering
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Mechatronics Engineering
