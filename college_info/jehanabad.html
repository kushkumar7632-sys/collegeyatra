Government Engineering College, Jehanabad
About College

Government Engineering College (GEC), Jehanabad is a government engineering institution established in 2019 under the Department of Science and Technology, Bihar. It is affiliated with Bihar Engineering University and approved by AICTE. The college aims to provide quality technical education and develop skilled engineers for the future.

Available Branches
  • Computer Science Engineering
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Mechatronics Engineering
