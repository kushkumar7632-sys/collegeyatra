Government Engineering College, Jamui
About College

Government Engineering College Jamui is a government engineering college in Bihar, established in 2019 under the Department of Science and Technology, Government of Bihar. The college is affiliated with Bihar Engineering University, Patna, and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). It is located in Jamui district, Bihar.

Available Branches
  • Computer Science Engineering
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Mechatronics Engineering
