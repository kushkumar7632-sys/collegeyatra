Government Engineering College, Siwan
About College

Government Engineering College Siwan (GEC Siwan) is a government engineering college located in Siwan. It was established by the Government of Bihar in 2018–19 to expand technical education across the state.
The college is approved by All India Council for Technical Education and affiliated with Bihar Engineering University, functioning under the Department of Science & Technology, Bihar.

Available Branches
  • Computer Science Engineering
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Mechatronics Engineering
