Bhagalpur College Of Engineering, Bhagalpur
About College

Bhagalpur College of Engineering (BCE), Bhagalpur is one of the oldest and most reputed government engineering colleges in Bihar, established in 1960 under the Department of Science and Technology, Government of Bihar. It is affiliated with Bihar Engineering University, Patna, and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). The college is located at Sabour, Bhagalpur, and has a large campus of around 230 acres, providing a strong academic and practical learning environment for students.

Available Branches
  • Computer Science Engineering
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Mechatronics Engineering
