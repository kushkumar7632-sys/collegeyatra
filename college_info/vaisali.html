Government Engineering College, Vaisali
About College

Government Engineering College Vaishali (GEC Vaishali) is a government engineering college located in Vaishali. It was established in 2018 by the Government of Bihar under the Department of Science & Technology to promote technical education in the state.
The college is approved by AICTE and affiliated with Bihar Engineering University, Patna.

Available Branches
  • Computer Science Engineering
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Mechatronics Engineering
