Saharsa College of Engineering
About College

Saharsa College of Engineering is a government engineering institution located in Saharsa. It was established in 2017 by the Department of Science and Technology Bihar to provide quality technical education in the region.
The college is affiliated with Bihar Engineering University, Patna and is approved by AICTE.

Available Branches
  • Computer Science Engineering
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Mechatronics Engineering
